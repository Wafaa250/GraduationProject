# SkillSwap — Orb Identity

**Tagline:** AI Talent Discovery & Collaboration Platform

**Original SkillSwap design** (circular orb mark + wordmark, no lettermarks inside the icon).

---

## Symbol

A **circular talent orb** (no tile, halo, or outer glow behind the circle):

- Radial purple → indigo inner sphere
- **Gold / amber** ring border (`#FDE68A` / `#FBBF24`)
- Inner **neural network** paths and nodes (white / soft violet)
- **Orange sparkle stars** as match accents (`#F59E0B`)

Reads as: AI matching · talent discovery · collaboration — not a university or chatbot icon.
